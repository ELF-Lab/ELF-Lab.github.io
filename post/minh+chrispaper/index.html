New Paper from Chris Hammerly and Minh Nguyen

April 7, 2025

ELF Lab members Chris and Minh have published a proceedings paper at the conference AmericasNLP on Ojibwe machine translation.

Nguyen, M. Hammerly, C. Silfverberg, M. (2025) A hybrid approach to low-resource machine translation for Ojibwe verbs. Proceedings of the 5th Workshop on Natural Language Processing for Indigenous Languages of the Americas (AmericasNLP 2025).
