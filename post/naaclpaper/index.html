New Paper from from ELF members and collaborators

April 7, 2025

ELF Lab members and collaborators, Shenran Wang, Changbing Yang, Mike Parkhill, Chad Quinn, Jian Zhu, and Chris Hammerly published a paper in the NAACL proceedings.

Wang, S., Yang, C., Parkhill, M., Quinn, C., Hammerly, C., and Zhu, J. (2025) Developing multilingual speech synthesis system for Ojibwe, Mi’kmaq, and Maliseet. In Proceedings of the 2025 Conference of the North American Chapter of the Association for Computational Linguistics (NAACL).
